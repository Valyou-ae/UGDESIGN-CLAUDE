## ğŸ”„ Iterative Improvement Prompts

If initial results aren't realistic enough, add these reinforcement phrases:

### For flat-looking designs:
```
"The design must show the natural undulation of the fabric surface - 
it cannot appear flat or 2D. Every micro-wrinkle affects the design."
```

### For colors too bright:
```
"The print colors should appear as they would from a real DTG printer - 
slightly muted by the fabric absorption, not digital-perfect colors."
```

### For missing fabric texture:
```
"The cotton weave pattern of the shirt must be subtly visible through 
the printed ink, especially in lighter colored areas of the design."
```

### For lighting mismatch:
```
"The printed design receives light from the exact same source as the 
rest of the garment - there should be zero lighting difference between 
printed and non-printed areas of the fabric."
```
